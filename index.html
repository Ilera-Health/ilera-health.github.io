About Us

Obianuju Makinde-Agu, MSN, PMHNP, BC

Welcome! I am Obianuju Makinde-Agu, a board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner (FNP) and Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP) with more than 15 years of experience in the field. I specialize in providing comprehensive psychiatric care through telehealth, offering a convenient and confidential way to receive mental health support from the comfort of your own home.

I utilize a combination of psychopharmacology, psychotherapy, and holistic approaches to treat a wide range of mental health conditions. My goal is to help you achieve mental wellness and improve your quality of life.
