About Us

At IleraHealth, we specialize in providing holistic mental health care. Our team of board-certified psychiatrists, therapists, and counselors is committed to helping individuals overcome challenges, improve emotional well-being, and lead fulfilling lives.

With years of experience and a compassionate approach, we combine evidence-based practices with personalized care to meet each client's unique needs. Whether you're seeking therapy, medication management, or lifestyle guidance, we are here to help.

Our Services

  • Individual Therapy: Personalized one-on-one sessions to address anxiety, depression, and more.
  • Medication Management: Expert guidance in managing psychiatric medications.
  • Telehealth Appointments: Convenient virtual sessions from the comfort of your home.
